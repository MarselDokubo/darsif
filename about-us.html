About DARSIF

Divine African Relief Services Inc (DARSI) is a foundation dedicated to bringing hope and support to the needy and less privileged across Africa. Our mission is to provide relief materials and empower communities by collaborating with organizations and individuals committed to uplifting those in need. With a focus on compassion, integrity, and impact, DARSI works tirelessly to create opportunities for change and alleviate the struggles of underserved populations.

Over the past five years, DARSI has conducted transformative programs, including the widely successful Feed the 500 initiative, which provided food and essential supplies to countless individuals and families. Through strategic partnerships and a dedicated team of volunteers, we have touched the lives of thousands across multiple African nations. Our commitment to impactful outreach continues to drive our efforts to build a brighter, more equitable future for vulnerable communities.

At DARSI, we believe in "hope for the hopeless" and extend unwavering support to those who cannot support themselves. Guided by this ethos, our foundation operates with transparency, ethical standards, and a shared vision of creating a lasting difference. Join us as we continue our journey to bring relief, restore dignity, and inspire positive change across Africa.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission at DARSI Foundation is to combat hunger and diseases delivering medical aids to communities across Africa. By addressing immediate need through food, healthcare and emergency support fostering long-term solutions through sustainable practices and education. DARSI envisions a future where all individuals have access to basic necessities and the opportunity to lead healthier, more secure lives.
